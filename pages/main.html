How It Works

FindMeASchool WORKS IN 4 DIFFERENT WAYS.

1- SEARCH:

Its simple. On FindMeASchool homepage, enter the name of a school you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all schools.

2- LOCATE:

Get to see where the school is located by clicking on the school profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

3- APPLY:

Apply for your child's interview to the schol of your choice. Click on the APPLY button to get the application form. We also follow up on the applicaton to ensure you get a reply on time.

4- RELAX:

Relax and wait for your child to be admitted. You can check our blog to see what we recommend when school hunting.
